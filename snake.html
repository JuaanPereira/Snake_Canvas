KEYS TO MOVE: W - A - S - D or ArrowUp - ArrowLeft - ArrowDown - ArrowRight

- HOW TO PLAY: Move the snake along the board avoiding collisions with the walls of the borders or with the snake itself, every single time you eat the fruit (yellow circle) snake will increase it's size and at the same time your score will increase aswell.

- IMPORTANT: Every 50 points the game speed will increase.
